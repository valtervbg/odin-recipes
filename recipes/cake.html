Cake

Description

This is a delicious cake recipe that is perfect for any occasion. It is moist, fluffy, and full of flavor. You can customize it with your favorite frosting and toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the milk, eggs, and melted butter to the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Once cooled, frost and decorate the cake as desired. Enjoy!
