Pizza

Description

A classic pizza with a crispy crust and delicious toppings. This easy-to-make pizza is perfect for a quick meal or a fun family activity.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni, sliced
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add the pepperoni and mushrooms on top.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
