Salad

Description

A fresh and healthy salad perfect for any meal. This simple salad is packed with nutrients and can be customized with your favorite ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup olives, pitted

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the mixed greens thoroughly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the greens, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, feta cheese, and olives.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
